Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Lumière Studios ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or submit a booking inquiry.

Information We Collect

When you use our booking form, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event date and service type
  • Optional budget range and project details

How We Use Your Information

We use inquiry information solely to respond to your request, prepare proposals, and coordinate photography or videography services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Form Processing

Booking submissions may be processed through Formspree or a similar secure form service to deliver messages to our team. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your light/dark theme preference. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our site may load content from trusted third parties such as Google Fonts, Google Maps, and Unsplash for fonts, maps, and portfolio imagery. These services have their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry details only as long as needed to provide services and meet legal or business requirements.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at hello@lumierestudios.co.ke.
