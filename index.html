Our Story

Where Art Meets
Authentic Emotion

Founded in Nairobi, Lumière Studios has become Kenya's most sought-after visual storytelling agency. We blend editorial precision with raw, heartfelt narrative — crafting imagery that transcends the moment and becomes legacy.

From intimate portrait sessions to grand corporate galas and destination weddings across East Africa, our team of master photographers and cinematographers deliver world-class results with uncompromising attention to detail.

"Our mission is to elevate every frame into art — preserving not just how things looked, but how they truly felt."
